About Robin Pearson

Robin Pearson is a BMX photographer living in London and working all over the world. He shoots BMX riders for fun and professionally at Ride BMX UK, and only ever use's film. When not taking photos, he is studying a for a BA in Sonic Art, playing in Hold Your Horse Is and riding BMX.
